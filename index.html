War of the Cybertrons

Turn-based Board Game

  • Warriors are placed on the battlefield at random.
  • Each is equipped with a weapon by default.
  • Everyone can make a move up to 3 squares.
  • If a player passes over a square containing a weapon, he leaves his current weapon there and replaces it with the new one.
  • Weapons have different damage points (power).
  • When the two warriors are side by side, a fight begins.
  • The damage inflicted depends on the weapon possessed by the player.
  • The player can choose to attack or defend against the next hit.
  • When the player defends himself, he takes 50% less damage than normal.
